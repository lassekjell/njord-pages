Vessel Motion — Roll, Pitch & Heave

Northern Gale — Catamaran survey vessel

Roll

Rotation about longitudinal axis (X)

Side-to-side tilting. The dominant motion on Northern Gale. Driven by beam seas and the catamaran's high metacentric height (GM).

Mild: ±6.5°
Rough: ±12.9°
Period: ~4 s

Pitch

Rotation about transverse axis (Y)

Bow up/down tilting. Well-controlled in moderate conditions due to the catamaran's long waterline. Increases sharply in head seas.

Mild: range 3.1° (+1° trim)
Rough: range 14.6°
Factor: 4.7×

Heave

Vertical translation (Z)

Vertical bodily movement — the whole vessel rises and falls with the waves. Critical for survey equipment and heave compensation.

Mild: ±0.45 m
Rough: ±1.36 m
Factor: 3.0×
